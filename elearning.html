New York University

This training is intended for patient recruitment facilitators, designed to build motivational interviewing skills with long term goals of improving patient participation in clinical trials.

As the Instructional Designer for this project, I worked with subject matter experts and graphic designers to develop the content, I also created complimentary activities and produced the 3 module course with Adobe Captivate.

This course trains participants on motivational interviewing strategies through interactive content. Students participate in various activities to assess and practice fundamentals of motivational interviewing, including an audio case study where students identify the MI strategies that are being employed.
